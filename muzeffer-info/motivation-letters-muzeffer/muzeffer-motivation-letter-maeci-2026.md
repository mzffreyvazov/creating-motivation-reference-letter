# Motivation Letter — MAECI Scholarship

**To:** MAECI Scholarship Committee

I am writing to express my strong interest in the MAECI Scholarship to fund my Master of Science in Data Science at Sapienza University of Rome. My application for the program is currently undergoing the pre-selection process.

I am a final-year Computer Science student at Azerbaijan State University of Economics with a 3.7/4.0 GPA. While my university studies gave me a solid technical grounding, my real passion for data science developed outside the standard curriculum. To satisfy my curiosity, I joined the Advanced Computer Science Program organized by IDDA in partnership with Constructor University Germany. Later, I also completed a specialized Data Science Program led by IDDA, StrategEast, and Sigma Software University. These experiences, along with earning my Oracle Cloud Infrastructure Data Science Professional certification, gave me the mathematical and programming foundations needed for Sapienza.

This interest was practically strengthened during my AI Engineer internship at 4SİM (Azerbaijan’s Centre for the Fourth Industrial Revolution). There, I built a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) chatbot for the national legal portal, e-qanun.az, to make complex legal databases easy for the public to navigate. Developing this AI system showed me firsthand how machine learning can directly solve real problems and improve public services.

The MSc Data Science at Sapienza appeals to me because the Computer Science department is recognized as a national Department of Excellence for machine learning. I am particularly drawn to the interdisciplinary structure and labs like HERCOLE, which focuses on explainable AI—something I believe is essential for public-sector technology. Italy offers a rich academic environment, and studying at Sapienza provides the ideal setting to work on innovative, real-world data problems before taking those skills further.

After graduation, my plan is to return to Azerbaijan and contribute to data-driven governance and tech initiatives. Building on my work at 4SİM, I want to use the methodologies I learn in Italy to advance our digital transformation, creating a lasting technological bridge between Italy and Azerbaijan. The MAECI scholarship would remove my financial barriers, allowing me to focus entirely on my studies so I can return home ready to make a meaningful impact.

Sincerely,

Müzəffər Eyvazov
