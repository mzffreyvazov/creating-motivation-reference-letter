# Program Research Dossier — MSc Computer Science and Data Analytics (2026/2027)

**Institution:** ADA University (joint degree with George Washington University)
**Program type:** MSc (Dual Degree — M.S. in Data Analytics from ADA + M.S. in Computer Science from GWU)
**Location:** Baku, Azerbaijan (Years 1–1.5) → Washington, D.C., USA (Semesters 4–5)
**As of date:** April 8, 2026

## 1) Executive Summary (5–8 bullets)

- The MSCS/DA is a **fully dual-degree program**: graduates receive both an M.S. in Data Analytics from ADA University and an M.S. in Computer Science from George Washington University (GWU) — two internationally recognized degrees in one enrollment.
- The program spans **five semesters**: three in Baku at ADA, two in Washington, D.C. at GWU's Foggy Bottom campus, providing genuine international academic and professional exposure.
- It is **fully funded for Azerbaijani nationals** through the State Program, covering tuition at both institutions, travel, and D.C. living costs; students pay only a 1,500 AZN annual non-refundable fee. Funding is not available for international applicants.
- The program is **research-centric**: it concludes with a master's thesis and explicitly targets candidates who demonstrate research interest or innovation through academic projects, publications, or work experience.
- The admissions pipeline is **four stages**: online application → ADA internal exam (multiple-choice, CS/math topics) → interview at ADA → GWU application (ADA nominees waive the GWU application fee).
- **Key deadlines for Fall 2026 intake:** First deadline April 8, 2026; second (late) deadline June 1, 2026.
- The MSCS/DA Class of 2025 cohort profile: 17 students, 24% female, **53% employed** at time of publication, average age 22, representing 5 universities across 4 prior majors; 82% were fresh graduates — signaling the program heavily favors recent BSc holders.
- The program is housed in ADA's **School of IT and Engineering (SITE)**, which explicitly prioritizes Big Data research, AI applications, and techno-entrepreneurship as its three strategic research pillars.

## 3) Curriculum & Academic Experience

- **Structure:** 5 semesters (~2.5 years); Semesters 1–3 at ADA University in Baku; Semesters 4–5 at GWU in Washington, D.C. Concludes with a master's thesis.
- **Core modules (representative — based on ADA's published curriculum PDF dated Oct 2024 and GWU's MS Data Analytics requirements):**
  - *At ADA (Baku semesters):* Advanced Algorithms, Machine Learning, Database Systems, Probability & Statistics for Data Science, Data Structures, Programming for Data Analytics
  - *At GWU (D.C. semesters):* Per GWU's MS Data Analytics (30 credits): 15 credits of required courses + 15 credits in either a Computer Science track or Engineering Management & Systems Engineering track — covering topics in computer science, business analytics, and systems engineering
  - Students choose a GWU track: **Computer Science** or **Engineering Management & Systems Engineering**
- **Thesis/Capstone:** A master's thesis is mandatory, conducted under joint supervision of ADA and GWU faculty. The thesis enables exploration of a research or professional topic in depth, and is designed to build a foundation for doctoral study or R&D careers.
- **Practical components:** Hands-on real-world project work at GWU's Science & Engineering Hall laboratories; GWU campus access including research centers and institutes.
- **Distinctive elements:**
  - **Dual degree** — two MSc degrees from two universities in one program; no parallel program of this type exists in Azerbaijan
  - **U.S. Embassy-endorsed** — the U.S. Embassy in Azerbaijan publicly endorsed the program as "playing an important role in bringing together Azerbaijanis and Americans"
  - **ADA Washington Center** — ADA maintains a four-floor building near Dupont Circle, D.C., providing institutional support for students during the U.S. semesters
  - **SITE school research pillars:** Big Data research, AI applications, techno-entrepreneurship (students join an ecosystem that explicitly centers these themes)
- **Evidence (links):** [Curriculum PDF (Oct 2024)](https://www.ada.edu.az/media/2024/10/27/computer_science_data_analytics_curriculum_24.pdf), [GWU MS Data Analytics](https://cs.engineering.gwu.edu/master-science-data-analytics), [GWU bulletin](https://bulletin.gwu.edu/engineering-applied-science/engineering-management-systems-engineering/data-analytics-ms/), [Program brochure (Mar 2025)](https://www.ada.edu.az/media/2025/03/12/site_mscs_da_updated.pdf)

## 5) Admissions & Eligibility (Hard Requirements)

- **Academic:** Bachelor's degree required. Minimum GPA **3.0/4.0 (equivalent to 80/100)**; ADA students/alumni may apply with GPA of 2.8. [Program page](https://www.ada.edu.az/en/schools/site/programs/computer-science-and-data-analytics)
- **Background/prerequisite knowledge:**
  - Mathematics: at minimum two courses beyond Calculus (e.g., Linear Algebra, Probability & Statistics)
  - Computer Science: Programming I & II, Discrete Mathematics, Data Structures, Algorithms
  - Demonstrated research interest or innovation (academic projects, publications, or relevant work experience) [Program page](https://www.ada.edu.az/en/schools/site/programs/computer-science-and-data-analytics)
- **Language:** TOEFL iBT 100 (or iBT score of 5 for exams taken on/after Jan 21, 2026) **OR** IELTS 7.0 **OR** PTE 68 **OR** Duolingo 130. IELTS "One Skill Retake" accepted. Home/online PTE and iBT not accepted — must be taken at registered testing center. Waivers for native English speakers or graduates of English-medium programs in English-official-language countries. [Program page](https://www.ada.edu.az/en/schools/site/programs/computer-science-and-data-analytics)
- **Documents (ADA application):**
  - Online application form (ada.edu.az/apply)
  - CV/Resume
  - Statement of Purpose (minimum 300, maximum 500 words — describing motivation, future goals, career aspirations) [Graduate Admissions page](https://www.ada.edu.az/en/admission/graduate)
  - Bachelor's diploma translated into English and notary-certified
  - Official transcripts
  - English proficiency test score
  - Application fee: 100 AZN (non-refundable)
- **Additional documents (GWU application — after passing ADA's internal exam stage):**
  - Statement of Purpose (max 400 words — GWU requirement) [GWU Partnership page](https://graduate.engineering.gwu.edu/ada-gw-partnership-program)
  - Bachelor's diploma (notary-certified English translation)
  - GWU application fee waived for ADA nominees
- **SEC test (State Examination Center):** Citizens of Azerbaijan and permanent residents must submit SEC master admission test results with a minimum score of 45. Registration is separate at the SEC website. [Graduate Admissions page](https://www.ada.edu.az/en/admission/graduate)
- **References:** Not explicitly listed as a separate requirement on official pages — **confirm directly with admissions office** (see Risks section).

## 6) Application Process & Deadlines

- **Deadlines (for Fall 2026 intake):**
  - **First deadline: April 8, 2026** (English test scores may be submitted up to the June 1 deadline even if application is submitted before April 8)
  - **Second (late) deadline: June 1, 2026**
  - [Program page](https://www.ada.edu.az/en/schools/site/programs/computer-science-and-data-analytics), [Graduate Admissions page](https://www.ada.edu.az/en/admission/graduate)
- **Evaluation process (4 stages):**
  1. **Online application** submitted to ADA University at ada.edu.az/apply
  2. **ADA Internal Exam** — multiple-choice questions in English covering CS and mathematics topics (a reading/topic list is published; candidates should check the program page for the current list)
  3. **Interview at ADA** — in English; Admission Committee evaluates academic records, intellectual potential, career focus, and readiness to succeed
  4. **GWU Application** — shortlisted candidates after ADA interview are invited to apply to GWU; ADA nominees are exempt from GWU's application fee. Admission is contingent on completing both ADA and GWU processes successfully.
- **Notes:**
  - This is NOT rolling admissions; there are two structured cutoff deadlines
  - Both ADA and GWU admission must be completed — failure at either stage means no admission
  - The GWU SoP has a stricter word cap (400 words) than ADA's SoP (up to 500 words)
  - SEC test must be submitted before the applicable deadline; candidates should register separately at the SEC website and monitor dates independently
  - [GWU Partnership page](https://graduate.engineering.gwu.edu/ada-gw-partnership-program)

## 7) Funding / Tuition / Cost

- **Tuition:** Fully funded for Azerbaijani nationals through Azerbaijan's **State Program**. Coverage includes: tuition at ADA University, tuition at GWU, travel costs to/from Washington, D.C., and monthly stipend + dormitory accommodation in D.C. [Program page](https://www.ada.edu.az/en/schools/site/programs/computer-science-and-data-analytics)
- **Student contribution:** 1,500 AZN per year (non-refundable annual fee) — the only direct cost to Azerbaijani-national students.
- **Previously government-funded graduates:** Azerbaijani nationals who have already completed a state-funded master's degree must self-fund ADA University tuition; travel and D.C. living costs remain state-covered.
- **International applicants:** Funding is NOT available. Tuition costs for international students are not published on the program page — **must be confirmed directly with admissions**.
- **Scholarships:** The Graduate Admissions page notes Graduate Assistantship awards for top applicants based on interview results and/or written exam. Eligibility and coverage details should be confirmed with admissions. [Graduate Admissions page](https://www.ada.edu.az/en/admission/graduate)
- **Cost of living in Baku:** No official guidance published. Baku is generally considered affordable by European/American standards. Student housing and transport costs are modest.
- **Cost of living in Washington, D.C.:** Covered by the State Program (stipend + dorm) for Azerbaijani-national students.
